About Me

My name is Jamal, and I’m a self-driven frontend developer and visual storyteller. My journey into tech started out of curiosity, and today, it has grown into a passion that drives me daily.

I specialize in building responsive web interfaces with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and Flutter. But I’m not just about code — my graphics design background gives me an eye for detail, layout, and color. This allows me to approach projects holistically, focusing on both how things work and how they look.

When I’m not writing code, you’ll probably find me:

  • Designing UI mockups
  • Watching tutorials or reading tech blogs
  • Experimenting with animations or transitions
  • Helping friends with their personal projects

I'm currently seeking opportunities to grow, connect with like-minded people, and work on real-world challenges. If you have something in mind, don’t hesitate to reach out.
